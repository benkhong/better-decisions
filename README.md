# How to Decide — A Working Manual

A single-file static HTML deliverable. Open `index.html` in any modern browser.
## What's inside

A field manual for thinking clearly and choosing well under uncertainty — in life, in work, and with money. Synthesized from Kahneman, Buffett, Munger, Tetlock, Taleb, Duke, Galef, Silver, Heath, Flyvbjerg, Henrich, and Ord.

**Five modules**

- **01 · Why your brain lies to you** — System 1/2, the Linda problem, base-rate neglect, anchoring (with interactive demo), the replication crisis
- **02 · Seeing clearly** — Bayes (interactive calculator), correlation vs causation, narrative fallacy, regression to the mean, fat tails, key biases (loss aversion calculator), calibration self-test, forecasting tournaments, outside view, scout mindset, steelmanning
- **03 · Choosing well** — Resulting, EV + Kelly (interactive sizer), one-way vs two-way doors, risk of ruin, ergodicity, sunk costs, kill criteria, optionality, the 70% rule, inversion, regret minimization, second-order effects, **what the great investors know** (Buffett, Munger, Graham)
- **04 · The toolkit** — Six 60-second tests, decision journal (printable), pre-mortem worksheet (printable), six worked examples, using a language model as a thinking partner
- **05 · The deeper game** — Incentives, Goodhart, game theory, Moloch, complex systems, evolution, antifragility, six meta-patterns that tie everything together

**Interactive widgets** — Anchoring demo · Bayes calculator · Loss-aversion ratio · EV + Kelly sizer · Calibration test

**Reference** — 30-entry glossary · 12-book reading list
